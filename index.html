Frequently asked questions

Is Swipio beginner-friendly?

Yes. The app is designed for quick starts and short daily sessions, even if you are new to language study.

How long should I study each day?

Most users see progress with 10-15 minutes daily. Consistency matters more than session length.

Does it support spaced repetition?

Yes. Cards are resurfaced when your memory is likely to fade, improving retention over time.

Can I learn on iPhone and iPad?

Yes, the app is optimized for iPhone and works smoothly on iPad as well.
